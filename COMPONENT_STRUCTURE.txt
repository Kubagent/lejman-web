DATA FLOW
=========

Mock Data (mockArtworks)
    ↓
Page Component (fetch by slug)
    ↓
ArtworkDetail Component (display & manage state)
    ↓
Lightbox Component (when triggered)


STATE MANAGEMENT
================

ArtworkDetail Component:
  • lightboxOpen: boolean
  • selectedImageIndex: number

Lightbox Component:
  • currentIndex: number
  • isZoomed: boolean
  • touchStart: number | null


RESPONSIVE BREAKPOINTS
======================

Mobile:   < 768px    → Edge-to-edge images, 24px padding
Tablet:   768-1023px → 48px margins, contained images
Desktop:  > 1024px   → 96px margins, max 960px images


NAVIGATION FLOW
===============

Archive Page (/works)
    ↓ (Click artwork card)
Individual Artwork (/works/[slug])
    ↓ (Click image)
Lightbox (full-screen viewer)
    ↓ (Press ESC or click close)
Individual Artwork
    ↓ (Click back button)
Archive Page
